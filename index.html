Rockstar Academy

Summary:

Rockstar Academy is an Integrated Physical Education Center in Indonesia that provides comprehensive early childhood education and physical activity programs for children aged 6 months to 18 years.

1. Service Offerings:

  • Conducts over 120 high-quality classes weekly across more than 13 Academies throughout the Jakarta area.
  • Offers various programs, including Early Childhood Education, Gymnastics, Dance, Ballet, Cheerleading, Broadway, Contemporary Dance, Martial Arts, and Sports.
  • Programs designed with progression from beginner to advanced levels to encourage consistent student improvement.
  • Instructors range from national champions to international professionals worldwide.
  • All programs are supported by internationally certified curriculums.

2. Target Market

  • Parents with Children Aged 6 Months - 18 Years.
  • Parents seeking early childhood education programs valuing the physical and socio-emotional development of their children.
  • Parents prioritizing structured and high-quality physical education and activities for their children.
  • Parents residing or working in the Jakarta area and surroundings, given Rockstar Academy's presence in over 13 locations in Jakarta.
  • Children aged 7 and above, as well as teenagers, searching for diverse classes, events, and competitions to nurture their interest in specific physical activities.

3. Kompetitor:

  • Superprof.co.id
  • Kiddo.id
  • Kidsrepublic.sch.id
